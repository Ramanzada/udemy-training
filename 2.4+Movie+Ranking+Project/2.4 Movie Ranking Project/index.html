The Best Movies Accroding to Raman

My Top 3 movies of all-time

Infinity Pool:

Directed by Brandon Cronenberg, "Infinity Pool" is praised for its blend of satire, psychedelic sexuality, and sci-fi body horror. The film's commitment by stars like Mia Goth and Alexander Skarsgard to their roles provides a unique hallucinogenic clarity to its narrative. It is notable for its "Cronenbergian perversion," offering a visceral experience for audiences seeking something beyond conventional horror

Talk to Me:

This film represents a blend of teen horror excitement with deeper emotional violence, reflecting the current transitional state of the horror genre. Directed by the RackaRacka duo, it brings an "anything-can-happen" feeling back to horror, engaging audiences with its dark themes including suicide and psychosis, while still providing a thrilling experience

Evil Dead Rise:

Part of the "Evil Dead" franchise, this installment stands out for being a fun and over-the-top bloodbath set in an almost deserted apartment building. It's lauded for delivering everything fans could hope for, while still pushing the franchise forward, proving the series' enduring appeal and versatility in delivering quality horror
